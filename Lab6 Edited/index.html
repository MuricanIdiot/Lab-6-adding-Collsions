X-Men Escape Game

_____________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Rules: Escape and DO NOT hit the lava. Remember, you are the Wolverine.

If you die, you will respawn to heal. Collect all coins to continue.

Coins will be added on next update. Brought to you and pixels drawn by Cindy Haslam.

_____________________________________________________________________________________________________________

To go left, press the Left arrow key.

To go Right, press the Right arrow key.

To Jump, press the Up arrow key.
